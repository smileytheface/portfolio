Ortiz Technical Management
This logo is the product of a conversation I had with a friend of mine about his technology management company, "ZTM". I asked him if he had a logo yet, and he said he would consider it down the line. I asked him if he wanted me to make him and logo and he kindly refused. My stubborn self made some mockups for him anyways and, ultimately, this was the logo we settled on.
BB
Me and a friend of mine came across a neat font online and we thought the B looked kinda cool, so I decided I would use it to make him a logo based on his nickname, "BB". Unfortunately, the font wasn't available for download, so I decided I would just recreate the B. After trying my best to make a half decent B and adding some fancy colors, we were able to come up with the shnazy logo to the left. Also looks real juicy in a dark theme...
Classic Hardwood Floors
This is a mockup logo for my friend's hardwood flooring business, Classic Hardwood Floors. It was one of my first logos, so it holds a special place in my heart.
Split Bun
